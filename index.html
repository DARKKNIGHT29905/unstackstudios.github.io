About Sport Pass

Our Sports Pass is a unified digital access solution for gyms, sports facilities, and fitness events nationwide. It simplifies membership management and connects users to a growing network of fitness opportunities — all through one smart pass.

We're building a seamless experience for both fitness enthusiasts and facility owners, making fitness more accessible and manageable than ever before.

Key Features & Benefits

🏟️

Comprehensive Venue Management

Manage multiple facilities, courts, and equipment from a single dashboard. Track maintenance schedules, monitor facility usage, and optimize resource allocation with our intelligent management system.

📅

Advanced Booking System

Smart scheduling engine that prevents double bookings, optimizes time slots, and allows customers to book facilities 24/7. Automated confirmations and reminders keep everyone informed.

💳

Integrated Payment Processing

Secure payment gateway supporting multiple payment methods. Automated billing, membership management, and financial reporting make revenue tracking effortless.

👥

Customer Management Portal

Comprehensive user profiles, booking history, loyalty programs, and personalized recommendations. Build stronger relationships with your customers through data-driven insights.

📊

Real-time Analytics & Reporting

Detailed insights into facility usage, peak hours, revenue trends, and customer behavior. Make informed decisions with our comprehensive analytics dashboard.

📱

Mobile-First Experience

Responsive design ensures seamless experience across all devices. Native mobile apps for both facility managers and customers coming soon.
